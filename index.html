Formula SAE

Formula SAE is a student design competition organised by SAE International. The concept behind Formula SAE is that a Fictional manufacturing company has contracted a student design team to develop a small Formula style race car. The prototype race car is to be evaluated for its potential as a production item.

Each student team designs,builds and tests a prototype based on a series of rules,whose purpose is both ensuring on track safety (the cars are driven by the students themselves) and promoting clever problem solving .The prototype race car is judged in a number of different events.
